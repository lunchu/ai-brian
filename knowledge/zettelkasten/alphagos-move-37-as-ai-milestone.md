# AlphaGo's Move 37 as AI milestone

AlphaGo's 37th move against Lee Sedol was initially considered an error by human experts but proved to be a brilliant strategic decision that won the match, marking the beginning of the modern AI era according to DeepMind CEO Demis Hassabis.
