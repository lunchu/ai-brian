# Google's Platform 37 London headquarters

Google's new London HQ named "Platform 37" after AlphaGo's famous move, spanning 330 meters (longer than The Shard), designed by Thomas Heatherwick and Bjarke Ingels, opening summer 2026 as Google's first fully-owned facility outside the US.
